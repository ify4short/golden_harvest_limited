About Us

Our Story


GoldenHarvestAcres Ltd is a sustainable agribusiness focused on transforming Nigeria's agricultural sector. We believe in creating wealth and jobs through sustainable practices while ensuring food security and environmental stewardship.


Our vision is to become Africa's leading sustainable agribusiness, creating wealth and jobs through oil palm and crop production for local and global markets, while driving food security, environmental stewardship, and global impact.


"Growing sustainably, empowering communities, and delivering value globally."

Sustainable Practices

Our Commitment to Community & Environment


At GoldenHarvestAcres, we believe that sustainable agriculture goes beyond just farming practices. It's about creating a positive impact on the communities we serve and preserving the environment for future generations.


Through our operations, we aim to reduce Nigeria's dependence on imported palm oil and other agricultural products, while creating meaningful employment opportunities for local communities.
